Foundation Course: Grow with Nature

Ages 6-12 learn agriculture, entrepreneurship, and sustainability through hands-on experience from seeds to harvest.

Foundation Course: Grow with Nature

Target Students: Ages 6–12 | Duration: 4 Weeks (Pilot Program) | Locations: Village Plots, Showrooms in Uchana & Narwana

At Swasia, we believe learning is best when hands, hearts, and minds work together with nature.

4-Week Course Journey

Week 1

Meet the Land

Soil exploration, composting, planting fast-growing seeds, making plant labels

Week 2

Water & Care

Watering techniques, indoor planting, building mini water-saving systems

Week 3

Harvest & Make

Harvesting greens/herbs, drying, grinding, packaging for sale

Week 4

Share & Reflect

Mini-market setup, showcase products, reflection, feedback

Daily Schedule (3 Hours)

Morning Circle (15 min)

Share observations and nature stories

Hands in Soil (30-45 min)

Plant, water, and care for plots

Creative Hour (30 min)

Eco-products, labels, or crafts

Reflection Journal (15-30 min)

Note observations and ideas

Mini Workshops / Market

Sell or showcase products (as scheduled)
